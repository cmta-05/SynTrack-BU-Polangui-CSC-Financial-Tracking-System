YOUR CONCERNS

File a concern if your payment is missing, incorrect, or not yet verified.

  • Click "File a Concern" in SynTrack.
  • Select the type of issue you want to report, such as:
    • Missing payment
    • Wrong amount posted
    • Not yet verified
    • Wrong module posted (e.g., Merchandise vs CSC Fee)
  • Provide the necessary details, such as payment proof, date, and module affected.
  • Submit the concern.
  • Wait for the CSC Treasurer or Business Manager to review and verify your issue.
  • Check your notifications for updates or resolution.
  • Done! Your concern is officially logged and tracked in SynTrack.
