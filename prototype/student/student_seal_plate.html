1. Walk-In Purchase
  • Check the Seal & Plate Section in SynTrack for available sets, price, and stock status.
  • Go to the CSC Office and pay directly in cash to the CSC Business Manager.
  • Receive your official receipt after payment.
  • Claim your Seal & Plate immediately, if available, or follow the instructions provided by the CSC Business Manager.
  • Your transaction will be recorded and verified in SynTrack (Status: Purchased, Paid & Verified).
  • Done! Keep your receipt as proof of payment.
2. Block or Pre-Order
  • Check the Seal & Plate Section in SynTrack for order availability and price.
  • Inform your Block Treasurer of your order and submit the cash payment.
  • Wait for the official receipt from your Block Treasurer.
  • The CSC Business Manager records and verifies your order in SynTrack:
    • Order Placed â€“ Pending Payment Verification
    • Paid & Verified
    • Processing (Seal & Plate is being prepared)
    • Ready for Pickup
  • Claim your Seal & Plate on the announced schedule at the CSC Office, presenting your Student ID and official receipt. In some cases, the Block Treasurer may be authorized to claim all Seal & Plates for the block, depending on official CSC instructions.
  • Done! You may return to this section anytime to view your order and payment history.

  • Maximum of 5 seals/plates per student per semester.
  • No lending to non-CSC organizations.
  • Damaged items must be surrendered before requesting a replacement.
