Project Overview

This prototype is part of the System Integration and Architecture (SIA) course and serves as a front-end simulation of the SynTrack – Bicol University Polangui College Student Council Financial Tracking System. The goal of this system is to streamline the recording, monitoring, and verification of CSC-related payments, including CSC Fees, Merchandise, PE Uniforms, Seal & Plates, and ID Lace transactions. All features are focused on transparency, ease of access, and supporting CSC financial operations.
